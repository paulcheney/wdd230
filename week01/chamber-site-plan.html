Site Name

The site name I have chosen for this assignment is "Thayne Chamber of Commerce" because it is the small town where I spent some time as a kid.

Site Purpose

We champion all businesses located in the greater Thayne area which includes shopping local.

We seek a unified voice in setting policy that affects taxes and government oversite.
